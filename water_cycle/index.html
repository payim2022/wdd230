Evaporation

According to wikipedia evaporation is a sort of vaporization that happens on a liquid's surface when it transitions into the gas phase. When humidity impacts the rate of evaporation of water, a high concentration of the evaporating component in the surrounding gas considerably slows evaporation. When liquid molecules collide, they exchange energy depending on how they collide.
